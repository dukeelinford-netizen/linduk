Internship Placement Website

The Internship Placement Website is a platform designed to assist students in finding suitable internship opportunities. The system provides information about available internships and allows students to apply for positions easily.

Objectives of the Website

The objectives of this website are to connect students with organizations offering internship opportunities, simplify the internship application process, and provide students with career development opportunities.

Target Users

The target users of this website include students seeking internship placements and organizations willing to offer internship opportunities.
